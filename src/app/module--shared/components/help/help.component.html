WELCOME TO FALCON SPORTS CLUB

Navigate to the Falcon Sports Club website and find the "Registration" section.

Select the category of sports you would like to register for - Individual Sports, Team Sports, or Dual Sports.

Choose the sporting event you want to participate in from the list of available options - Sprint race, Shooting, Swimming, Weight Lifting, Boxing, Cricket, Football, Tennis, or Cycling.

Fill in your personal details, such as name, organization name, Age group, sports category, sports information.

If registering for a team sport, provide the name and contact information of the other members on your team.

Agree to the terms and conditions of the event, including waiver of liability, medical consent, and code of conduct.

Once you have completed the registration process, you will receive a confirmation email with details about the event, including the date, time, and location.

The Falcon Sports Club will ensure that all registered participants receive adequate instructions, training, and equipment needed to participate in their chosen sport. The club will also provide a safe and welcoming environment for all participants, regardless of their skill level or experience. Participants can look forward to a thrilling and competitive experience that promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Eligibility:

Before registering for any event, participants must check the eligibility criteria for each sport category and event. These criteria may vary based on age, gender, and skill level.

Registration process:

The registration process for Falcon Sports Club events will be done online through the club's official website. Participants must create an account and fill out a registration form, providing accurate personal and contact information.

Team registration:

For team sports, participants must register as a team with a team name, captain, and other team members' details. Each team must have a minimum and maximum number of players, as specified in the event rules.

Event rules:

Each event will have its specific rules and regulations, such as equipment requirements, dress code, and match format. Participants must read and understand these rules before participating in the event.

Schedule:

The schedule for each event, including the event date, time, and venue, will be updated on the website once the registration process is complete.

Contact information:

In case of any queries or concerns regarding the registration process, participants can contact the Falcon Sports Club team through the website's contact form or customer support number.

Navigation Help

To navigate the sports category and available sports, follow these steps:

  1. Look for the navigation bar on the website or application.
  2. Locate the "Sports" category within the navigation bar.
  3. Click on the "Sports" category to view the available sports.

To find a participant from the participant list, do the following:

  1. Look for the "Participant" section on the website or application.
  2. Click on the "Participant" section to view the participant list.
  3. Search the participant list for the specific participant you are looking for.

To view the schedule fixture list and events, use the following steps:

  1. Find the "Schedule" section on the website or application.
  2. Click on the "Schedule" section to view the schedule fixture list.
  3. Search the fixture list for the specific fixture or event you are interested in.

To access the register form for participants and organizations, follow these steps:

  1. Locate the "Register" section on the website or application.
  2. Click on the "Register" section to view the registration form.
  3. Fill out the registration form with the required information for the participant or organization.

To view the details on sports categories, available organizations, and age group information, do the following:

  1. Look for the "Details" section on the website or application.
  2. Click on the "Details" section to view the available information.
  3. Search for the specific details you are interested in, such as sports categories, available organizations, and age group information.
